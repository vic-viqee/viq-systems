Problem examples

Types of problems I help businesses solve. These are real — you might recognize yours.

Problem type

Clients keep forgetting bookings

A service business gets tons of no-shows because clients forget their appointment. They're using WhatsApp reminders manually or nothing at all.

→ Solution: Automated booking system with reminders + a client portal to reschedule anytime

Problem type

Accounting is a nightmare

A small business collects payment via M-Pesa, cash, transfers, and card — all going to different places. At month-end, reconciliation takes days and the owner has no idea where money actually went.

→ Solution: A single dashboard that consolidates all payment types, auto-categorizes, and generates monthly reports in seconds

Problem type

Customer data is chaos

A business has customer info scattered across emails, WhatsApp, notes, and spreadsheets. Sales teams cannot see purchase history. You lose repeat business because you forget who they are.

→ Solution: A mini-CRM that centralizes all customer info, tracks purchase history, and triggers follow-up reminders

Problem type

Inventory is a guessing game

A retail shop loses sales because nobody knows what's in stock. Ordering is based on gut feel — you either run out of bestsellers or overstock what doesn't sell.

→ Solution: Simple inventory tracker with low-stock alerts, supplier lists, and sales reports that actually make sense

Problem type

WhatsApp orders are chaos

You take orders on WhatsApp but tracking which ones are paid, delivered, or still pending is a mess. Customers double-text asking for status and nothing is organised.

→ Solution: Order management system linked to M-Pesa confirmations, auto-status updates, and customer notifications
