Live project

FluxPay

Kenyan businesses that want to accept M-Pesa payments usually have to build the integration themselves — collections, recurring billing, reconciliation, all of it. FluxPay is a platform that handles that layer, so a business can plug in M-Pesa payments and subscription billing without building any of the underlying logic.

  • Accepts M-Pesa payments instantly through STK push
  • Manages recurring subscriptions and billing automatically
  • Gives the business a dashboard showing revenue, transaction trends, and customer activity at a glance
  • Works as subscription billing or a stand-alone payment gateway for retail and e-commerce
  • Exports transaction and customer data to a spreadsheet on demand
  • Sends automatic email notifications — password resets, account updates — with no manual follow-up needed

Concept example

Booking & deposits for a service business

The problem: Picture a high-end tattoo studio coordinating bookings over WhatsApp — checking availability by hand, sending payment details one by one, tracking deposits on a spreadsheet, and chasing no-shows with reminder texts.

What we'd build: A booking portal wired directly into the M-Pesa Till API. Clients pick an open slot, pay a deposit on the spot, and get an automatic confirmation. The calendar updates itself — nobody has to scroll a chat thread to know what's booked.

  • No-shows drop sharply once deposits and reminders are automatic
  • Zero manual follow-up for booking confirmations
  • Payments reconcile themselves — no more spreadsheet tracking
  • Clients reschedule on their own, no back-and-forth needed
Concept example

Live tracking for a logistics business

The problem: Picture a delivery business tracking every order through a chain of WhatsApp messages — dispatchers calling drivers for updates, status changes getting lost in chat, and end-of-day reconciliation done by hand against a paper log.

What we'd build: A web dashboard with live delivery tracking, automatic status updates sent straight to customers, and reports that generate themselves at the end of each day. Drivers get a simple one-tap mobile screen to update a delivery's status.

  • Hours of manual coordination removed every week
  • Customers get real delivery status without calling to ask
  • Daily and weekly reports generate themselves
  • Reconciliation drops from hours to minutes
