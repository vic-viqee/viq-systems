My approach

How I identify problems and build solutions.

I'm early in taking on client work, so this page shows the framework I use to approach business problems — and one completed project showing real problem-solving in action.

1. Discovery

You describe the headache. I ask questions to find the ROOT problem (not the symptom). Most business problems aren't what you think they are at first.

2. Build

I build ONLY what solves your problem. No extra features, no feature bloat, no "just in case" buttons. Simple. Focused. Works.

3. Verify

Does it solve the problem? More bookings? Fewer hours wasted? Less stress? That's the only metric that matters. Not prettier — better.
