What is Viq.tech?

The short answer, and the honest one.

Viq.tech is a one-person operation that builds websites, business systems, and automation tools for businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets. We work directly with owners and operators — no account managers, no handoffs, no agency bloat.

The name comes from Viqee — Victor's own nickname. Viq.tech isn't a faceless studio hiding behind a brand; it's one person who answers the phone, writes the code, and stays reachable after launch. Every project still has to pass one test: does it actually solve the problem? Time saved, money recovered, stress removed. If the answer isn't yes, we don't build it.

That's the whole philosophy. No tech for tech's sake. Just practical systems that make your business run better.
