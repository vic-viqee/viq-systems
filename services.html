Common questions

I'm not sure if my problem needs a custom system.

That's what the discovery call is for. We talk about what's broken, and I'll tell you honestly whether a custom system makes sense or if a cheaper solution would work better. I'm not trying to sell you something you don't need.

How long does it actually take?

Depends on complexity. A simple booking system: 2–4 weeks. A more complex system (accounting dashboard, CRM with integrations): 4–8 weeks. Timeline is agreed before we start.

What if I need changes after launch?

I don't disappear after launch. You get a post-launch support window included, and after that, ongoing maintenance is available as a monthly retainer if you want it.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes — about half my work is international. Payment is through Payoneer or PayPal for international clients, M-Pesa for local ones.

What if my problem is really custom and unusual?

That's exactly what I'm built for. Off-the-shelf tools fail on unusual problems. Custom systems are the whole point. Tell me what's weird about your situation — that's probably the most interesting part.

Still not sure?

Tell me what problem you are trying to solve. I'll tell you if I can help, and how much it would cost.
